Figure 1. caption describing above picture. Click the image to visit the app.

This app utilizes:

  • the Express framework

  • MongoDB

  • JavaScript:

    • NodeJS

    • ReactJS

    • CommonJS

To get it up and running, in top level of app run…​

npm start

Then get the database hooked up by running,

mongod --dbpath data

Finally, in yet another window, run,

mongo

Then open up your browser to http://localhost:2990.
